How we got started?

3 guys, a dream and a truck filled with ice cream. We essentially started with four flavors: vanilla, strawberry, chocolate and neapolitan. After our regular 9 - 5 jobs we would start making and mixing ice cream in the kitchen and then on the weekends we would load up our truck that was loaned to us by a friend that owned his own catering business, and hit the streets

After a few years of relentless persistence and determination and countless hours making ice cream, our dream would soon take flight, a few famous people tried our ice cream and loved it! Shortly afterwards those same famous people would post about The Ice Cream Shop on social media, and before we knew it we were getting more customers than we could handle, we immediately started hiring more employees to keep up with the rapid growing demand.

Our mission has always been to have fun and make ice cream that people know and love!
